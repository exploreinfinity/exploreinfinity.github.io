Arthur's Seat

Aug 6, 2021

In my first year at the University of Edinburgh, I lived in the Pollock Halls, where is really near to the Arthur's Seat. So I spent a lot of time on the mountain, relaxing, and enjoying the nature.

The scenery is super great, as you can see in the photos on the left. On top you can see a small portion of the city, which is better looking at night, when the lights are on. The way leads to the Arthur's Seat top is also very beautiful, and I was just totally attracted by the beauty of the nature there.

Calton Hill

Aug 6, 2021

Another worth-going place in Edinburgh is, of course, Calton Hill. It is famous for a collection of monuments and buildings.

Calton Hill let me feel the power of human and the beauty of architecture. It also reminds me how rich the history of Edinburgh really is. Just like Arthur's Seat, you can overlook a small portion of the city from the hill. It is said that it would be more beautiful at dusk when the golden light cast on the monuments, but unfortunately, I haven't been there at dusk, so maybe next time!
